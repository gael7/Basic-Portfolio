About Me

Hi everyone, as you may notice my name is Gael Alberto Arrambide, but please call me by my first name, Gael. I was born in Nuevo Laredo, Tamaulipas, Mexico. I became a US citizen when I was 17 years old. All my education was given in Mexico, with the exemption of two semesters between 2009 and 2010 where I took some classes at Laredo Community College. I graduated as a Mechatronic Engineer at Instituto Tecnologico de Nuevo Laredo on 2014.

While at university I participated on 3 national robotics contests called "Torneo Mexicano de Robotica" on different cities around Mexico like Ciudad de Mexico, Puebla, and Ciudad del Carmen. I have retail experience on Ueta Duty Free and Walmart.
